Installation

Instructions:

  1. Download the latest version of Qlang from the Github page (for your OS).
  2. Unzip the .zip archive.
  3. Move the folder with the compiler to a convenient location on your computer.

  5. Clap your hands and congratulate yourself on a job well done.
